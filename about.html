We produce or supply Goods, & Services

We Incepted in the year 1981, we are engaged in offering a wide range of Pickling Passivating Services on Stainless Steel Pipes, Tanks, Vessels, Sheets, Bars, Angles, Tubes, Strip, Welds Joints, etc. We also provide SS Casting Pickling and Passivation, Carbon Steel Pickling, Super Duplex Stainless Steel Cleaning, Metal Surface Treatment and Buffing Job Works. We work on stainless steel 304, 316, 410, 321, 304L, 316L, ASTM A312 and Super Duplex grade. Many different industries need pickling passivation services including manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, spacecraft, food, kitchenware, cutlery, medical, pharmaceutical, healthcare, oil & gas, petrochemical, construction, engineering, energy and many more.
